Necessary Component
The oil lines provide a constant supply of engine oil to lubricate the turbocharger bearings. This allows the shaft and turbine wheel to spin smoothly at up to 150,000+ rpm without excessive wear.
Blockage
Sludge, contaminants or debris can block the small oil supply passages inside the turbo, reducing oil flow.
Carbon Buildup
Oil coking from heat can leave carbon deposits that restrict oil flow within the turbo.
