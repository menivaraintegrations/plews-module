Conquer

Extreme Conditions

Edelmann Elite® High Performance Power Steering Hoses — the epitome of power steering hose innovation. Designed with advanced engineering techniques and an enhanced rubber formulation, Elite hoses excel in extreme temperatures, from freezing polar vortex conditions to scorching heatwaves.

10X

Corrosion
Resistance

Superior plating on Elite hoses means that the hoses will last longer in environments where water, salt, and other corrosive agents are present.

4X

Impulse
Durability

Tested to 1,000,000 hot impulse cycles without failure, elite hoses will lower maintenance needs on vehicles running longer and hotter over thousands of miles. Additionally, Elite hoses resist hardening and retain pliability in temperatures below -40° F for more confident start-ups in cold weather.

2X

Less
Swelling

Power steering fluid causes rubber to swell and this swelling degrades the hose over time.  With Elite hoses, the rubber material retains less fluid and cuts degredation by more than half.

Superior Range

One of the standout features of Edelmann Elite hoses is its superior all weather performance. No matter the climate, these hoses remain steadfast, providing outstanding impulse durability and stellar cold temperature pliability. With this remarkable line of hoses, drivers have the confidence and peace of mind, knowing that your power steering system is well-equipped to handle whatever nature throws its way.
