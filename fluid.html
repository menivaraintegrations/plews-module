Elite fluid

For Elite Systems

Our Edelmann Elite hoses are rated for extreme temperatures, but most fluids on the market can't keep up. So we provide the market with an Elite fluid to match our Elite products.

Universal Compatibility For Foreign and Domestic Vehicles

Protects power steering systems with advanced additives, preventing wear, leaks, and corrosion. Withstands extreme temperatures (-58°F to +400°F) for trouble-free operation. Trusted performance and ultimate protection for all power steering systems.  Works with all vehicle makes with the exception of Honda/Acura.

Optimum System Wear Prevention

Formulated with synthetic base stocks and advanced additive technology, Edelmann Elite® Synthetic Power Steering Fluid provides excellent wear protection for power steering systems, ensuring trouble-free operation even under severe conditions and extreme temperatures.

Corrosion and Oxidation Prevention

This power steering fluid is designed to prevent system corrosion and oxidation, safeguarding power steering pumps and hoses from damage and ensuring long-lasting performance.

Versatile Compatibility and Top-Off Capability

Edelmann Elite Power Steering Fluid is approved and compatible for top-off with all power steering fluids, making it a convenient choice for most foreign and domestic passenger cars and light trucks.
