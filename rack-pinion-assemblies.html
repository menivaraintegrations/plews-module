Magnasteer

Reinvented

Magnasteer uses an electromagnet and permanent magnet attached to the steering rack to vary the amount of steering effort needed based on vehicle speed.

Because of how Magnasteer racks are designed, they are notoriously difficult for any manufacturer to offer to the aftermarket, be it reman or new.

We overcame the difficulties of developing the technology and provided the aftermarket with newly manufactured Magnasteer-style racks for some late model GM vehicles.
