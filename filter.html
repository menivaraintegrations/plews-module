Smart Construction

Simple Prevention

Versatility is key with the Edelmann Elite® Universal Power Steering Filter.  It’s compatible with various hose sizes, thank to the interchangeable hose barbs.  The compact body design ensures a perfect fit in tight engine bays.  Whether conducting repairs or maintaining a functional system, adding the Edelmann Elite filter prevents mature failure and keeps vehicles on the road.

Reduce inventory

Only one filter needed for 99%+ vehicle coverage

With the inclusion of interchangeable end fittings, this one filter is able to provide coverage for 99%+ VIO in the market.  As long as there is a space along the path of the return hoses, this compact,universal filter will provide protection for virtually any vehicle on the road today.

clean fluid

Over time, the fluid inside a power steering system becomes its own worst enemy.  Dirty fluid leads to more contaminants and debris being formed from within the system.  As the particulate increases, the damage and potential system failure become more and more likely.  Removing the particulate at the start will prevent this cycle from even beginning.

Higher Capacity

Better Flow

The high-capacity steel mesh filter is nearly 3X as large as our competitors without sacrificing any filtration.  That is 3X the ability to remove particulates as well as 3X the flow.The filter screen is completely removeable, so it can be cleaned and replaced.  This allows the filter to be used indefinitely without having to replace the entire unit.

dual filtration

Remove More

Our dual filtration magnet is designed to remove more metal shavings than the competition.  Our magnetic filtration is 4x larger and stronger than our competition.The complete removal metal particulate is imperative due to the destructive effect of this material on the hose.  As the metal pieces flow through the hose, they scrape and remove rubber fragments from the hose which eventually build up inside the hard parts and cause failure.

retail ready packaging

Clamshell packaging makes it possible to display the filter on retail shelves on near the counter to promote the addtion of a filter to any power steering repair, even if fluid is the only purchase.
