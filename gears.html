Gears

By addressing common issues, such as cracking pistons, our enlarged pistons ensure optimal performance under extreme loads. For heavy-duty applications like towing, our gearboxes feature increased teeth size for improved strength. Additionally, upgraded seals effectively prevent fluid seepage and maintain lubrication, safeguarding the system from debris.

Enlarged Pistons

In some cases, OE pistons were undersized and prone to cracking.  We increased the size of the pistons to prevent cracking under extreme loads.

Heavy Uses

When vehicles are used for towing or carrying a heavy load, an increased strain is placed on the teeth in the gearbox.  Where possible, our gearboxes have increased the teeth size to improve strength and durability.

Increased Seals

Seals have been increased to prevent fluid from seeping past the pitman shaft seal under extreme loads.  This keeps the gear lubricated and prevents debris from entering the system unintentionally.
